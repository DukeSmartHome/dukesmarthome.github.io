About

The Duke smart home is a student organization at Duke University, focused on exploring sustainable living as well as the cutting-edge of smart home technology. The Duke Smart Home organization is comprised of two key components, a residential program, and a project-based student club.

Residential Program

The Smart Home residential program is dynamic and vibrant living and learning community. Every year, 10 students from Duke University are selected through an application process to become Smart Home residents. The application for the 2017-2018 school year will be released in early 2017.
